Mexican Spicy Beef Bowl

Description

One of my favorite meals. A delicious and spicy Mexican beef bowl. Easy to make and very nutritious.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground beef
  • 1 diced red onion
  • 1 diced garlic clove
  • 1-2 packs of taco seasoning of choice
  • 1 cup White rice
  • 1 cup Shredded cheese
  • 1 tbsp Coconut oil

Instructions

  1. Heat coconut oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Add diced red onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  4. Stir in taco seasoning and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add white rice and stir to combine.
  6. Cover and cook until rice is tender, about 10 minutes.
  7. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top and let it melt.
